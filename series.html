Series

You human readers might be use to the 3rd dimension, but there are actually thousands upon thousands, all in which are in Mr. Jenkins' belly! Muquesko an adult rattlesnake just had his wife and son kidnapped! His hope is that he can do whatever he can to find them and maybe make a few friends along the way!

"A Way to Understand" is about teaching children about differences and just because someone is different doesn't make them any less capable then another person. "A Way to Understand" does this with the two main characters Jacob who is Deaf and Kayla who is blind. They have talents, they have interests, they enjoy life just as anyone else would and being different doesn't change that.
