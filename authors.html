One tragic day struck when somebody kidnapped his wife and child. Muquesko then moved to Huqworld, located in Illinois, thank to Chubskey and Master Imono’s help they found Muquesko’s son. He’s still in search for his wife. It was Chubskey that got him into writing and drawing. Now Muquesko writes childrens books with Crush Canidae.
